Impact of COVID-19 on Economy of Australia

COVID-19 is not only a global pandemic, it is also a public health crisis that affect financial market and global economy significantly. Chain effect like reduction in income, a rise in unemployment, and disruptions in the transportation, service, and manufacturing industries are among the consequences of the disease mitigation measures that have been implemented in many countries. It has become clear that most governments in the world underestimated the risks of rapid COVID-19 spread and were mostly reactive in their crisis response. As disease outbreaks are not likely to disappear in the near future, proactive international actions are required to not only save lives but also protect economic prosperity.

Global GDP of 2020 Compared to 2019

Australia's GDP growth has reduce by 4.2% compared to last year, 2019. The number might seeem small but in term of GDP, -4.2% is actually really bad and it will cause a series of bad chain effect like reduction in income, then follow by unemployment and so on.

One of the major reason might be the travel ban and travel restriction. These restrictions have severly damanged the education, tourism and export industry (It affected other businesses as well). Unemployment Rate of States in 2020

Based on the choropleth map idiom on the right and the population size, Tasmania is heavily impacted by COVID-19 pandemic. The unemployment rate in Tasmania is 7.6% and that is 2.1% more compared to 2019. Beside that, Queensland get affected significantly with a 7.7% unemployment rate in 2020 so far. All the data is normalised and based on the population of each state.

The possible reason for the rising of unemplyment rate might due to the fact that businesses are either temporary closing or shut down due to the restriction and travel ban applied by the government because of the pandemic. To compensate with the closure, a lot of businesses have chosen to either reduce the amount of employee that is not crucial and reduce the income of overall hirerachy of employee in the company to cover the lost. Meanwhile, underemployment will take place where people are paid less than what they suppose to be paid for.
